Social Security Levy

 

Social Security Levy is another form of levy that the IRS can place on you. The IRS will send you a “Notice of Intent to Levy” or “Seizure of Social Security Income” thirty days before it actually happens. The Levy will be a percentage of your social security income and it can be anything between 15%-90%. This levy takes about 30 days to be released and/or taken into effect. You need to take action as soon as possible to avoid the IRS from taking your social security income.

Call The Tax Defense Group if you have any questions or need help if the IRS has sent you a notice of Seizure of Social Security Income.
